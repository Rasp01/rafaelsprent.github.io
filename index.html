About Me

Experienced GIS Specialist with a passion for leveraging geospatial technologies to drive efficient utility solutions. Currently, I am a part of the dynamic Utility Solutions Team at AtkinsRealis, where I contribute to cutting-edge projects that optimize infrastructure management and enhance operational performance.

In my role, I thrive in an agile workspace, collaborating closely with cross-functional teams to develop innovative solutions. My expertise lies in utilizing tools such as FME FORM, FME FLOW, ArcPY, and Microsoft SQL Databases to analyze, manipulate, and visualize geospatial data. By leveraging these technologies, I have successfully implemented robust spatial analyses, streamlined data workflows, and generated actionable insights.

Based in Victoria, London, I bring four years of valuable experience in GIS to my current position. Prior to joining AtkinsRealis, I earned a Distinction in my Masters in Geospatial Sciences from UCL, where I deepened my understanding of advanced geospatial techniques, data management, and spatial modeling.

Passionate about staying at the forefront of GIS advancements, I continuously expand my skillset through professional development opportunities and remain up-to-date with the latest industry trends. I am committed to driving sustainable and data-driven decision-making processes that optimize utility networks and contribute to the betterment of our communities.

Let's connect and explore how my GIS expertise can add value to your organization or discuss potential collaboration opportunities in the geospatial domain.
